Celebrating Bruce!

Bruce was born was on the 25th of April 1997, in Pietermaritzburg. From birth it was clear that Bruce was destined for great things. He grew up on a pig farm, called Fagazaan just outside Michaelhouse where he enjoyed pondering the meaning of life and treating his body like a temple. At then tender age of 13 Bruce then left home and went to boarding school at Balgowan high (AKA Michaelhouse) where he was in Tatham house.
